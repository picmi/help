# Previewing an opportunity

Use the preview pane to ensure values are as expected.

:::: instructions
## Preview details

1. Go to either:
   * **Employment** > **Jobs**
   * **Services** > **Proposals**
2. Locate the row :::icon checkbox-marked-outline:::
3. Use one of the various methods to show the Job edit details pane
   * Click on the row
   * Click &vellip; (vertical dots) to open the menu, select :::icon pencil-outline **Edit**:::
4. Locate **Preview & Access** section
5. Click :::icon eye-outline **Preview**:::
6. Click :::icon arrow-left **Back**:::, then **Done** when you're finished
::::

Also check that it is [valid and ready to be published](../guide/jobs/preparing-a-job-ready-publish.md)
